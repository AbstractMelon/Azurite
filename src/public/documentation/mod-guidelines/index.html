Mod Guidelines

Acceptable Content

  • Mods should enhance the gameplay experience without breaking the core mechanics.
  • All content must be original or have permission for use from the original creators.
  • Mods should not include offensive or inappropriate material.
  • Ensure mods do not conflict with the terms of service of the game they modify.

Submission Rules

  • Provide a detailed description of what your mod does.
  • Include installation instructions and any dependencies required.
  • Submit your mod in a compressed format (e.g., .zip or .rar).
  • All submissions must include a version number and changelog.
  • Ensure your mod is tested and functional before submission.

Best Practices

  • Maintain clear and organized code for ease of maintenance and updates.
  • Keep documentation up-to-date with any changes or updates.
  • Engage with the community for feedback and support.
  • Respect the intellectual property of other creators and seek collaboration where possible.
